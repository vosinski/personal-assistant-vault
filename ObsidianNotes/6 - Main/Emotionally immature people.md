# Emotionally immature people

Emotionally Immature People (EIPs) are individuals who exhibit a wide range of personality styles characterized by a lack of emotional maturity. Understanding EIPs is crucial for anyone dealing with them, particularly adult children of emotionally immature parents (ACEIPs), as it can provide a framework to understand past experiences and improve current relationships. This framework helps to decipher internal emotional and physical warning signals and articulate previously unconceptualized relational experience
