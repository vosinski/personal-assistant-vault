# Daily reflections 2025-09-12

I have observed that I have a cognitive distortion about adults:

My parents had formed the perception that adults are wise and know anything better that me.

And it leads to few consequences:
1. I was pretty sure that you just need to wait until you will grow to be wise and know everything better. To gain skill just need to wait some time.
2. I was sure that I'm not enough and it doesn't even worth to try to change something, because of somebody knows and does it much more better that you. 
3. And I always can rely on someone more adult and it means more wise
4. And also it forced me to accept unhealthy advices from person who seemed more adult
